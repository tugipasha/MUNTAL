About MUNTAL

Where tomorrow's leaders find their voice.

MUNTAL is an annual Model United Nations conference bringing together delegates from across Turkey to debate, negotiate, and craft resolutions on the world's most pressing challenges.

Hosted at Nedim Uysal Mesleki Ve Teknik Anadolu Lisesi in İzmir Atatürk Organize Sanayi Bölgesi, MUNTAL offers an immersive diplomatic experience — rigorous committee sessions, cultural exchange, and the camaraderie that only shared purpose can forge.

"The floor is yours, delegate."
